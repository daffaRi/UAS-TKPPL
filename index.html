About Us

Miss salon is an upscale beauty salon, located in the city center of Medan. Founded in 2018, Miss salon offers a wide range of beauty services, such as waxing, facials, manicures, and pedicures. In addition, the salon also provides makeup services and sells a variety of beauty products.

Miss salon also offers a variety of packages and deals to their customers. These deals are designed to make it easier for customers to get the treatments they want at a price that is affordable. In addition, the salon offers a loyalty program that rewards customers for their continued patronage.

Miss salon prides itself on its commitment to customer satisfaction. The staff are friendly and welcoming, and they always strive to provide the best possible experience for their clients. They also provide a range.

Our Mission

The mission of Miss salon is to provide their clients with the best possible beauty treatments and to make them look and feel their best. The staff of Miss salon are highly trained and experienced, and they are dedicated to providing their clients with the highest quality of service. They use only the best products and techniques available, so their customers can trust that they will always get the best results. Miss salon also strives to create a welcoming and comfortable environment for their customers and to ensure that each customer leaves feeling beautiful, confident, and satisfied.

Our Vision

The vision of Miss salon is to become the leading beauty salon in the region, providing exceptional services and products that exceed the expectations of our customers. We strive to create an environment that is welcoming, comfortable, and focused on providing our customers with the best possible experience. We want to be seen as a trusted resource for our customers, and to be their go-to destination when they want to look and feel their best. We are committed to excellence, and are dedicated to providing our customers with the highest quality of service and products.

History

The Chronological History of Miss salon

2018

2019

Miss salon expands its services to include waxing, facials, manicures, and pedicures.

2020

Miss salon launches its loyalty program and begins selling a variety of beauty products.

2021

Miss salon introduces an array of packages and deals for their customers.

2022

Miss salon begins offering makeup services and introduces a new range of products to their store.

2023

Miss salon continues to grow, offering more services and products to their customers.
